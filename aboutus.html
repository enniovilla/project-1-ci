Our Story

Founded in 2022 by artists Laura Thomas and Jennifer Lee, Musical Theatre Berlin (MTB) aims to bring the Broadway-style charm to Berlin's art scene with English-language performances for an international audience. MTB also focuses on promoting women and the LGBTQIA+ community's artistic expression, ensuring their contribution and visibility in its productions. MTB is committed to producing and showcasing professional performances, on top of offering workshops for aspiring singers, dancers, and actors.


MTB is a new arm of a very successful program in the UK, founded by Thomas more than 15 years ago. Plays like Chicago, The Wizard of Oz, Mamma Mia and Rent were results of workshops, in which everyone had the opportunity to go deep into the musical world and develop their artistic skills.

Our Team

Laura, our Founder and Director, is the driving force behind Musical Theatre Berlin, is a seasoned performer and educator from London with a passion for nurturing young talent. With extensive experience on renowned stages worldwide, she brings expertise and inspiration to the school. Known for innovative programs and a commitment to excellence, MTB under her leadership continues to shape aspiring performers into confident artists.

Bringing extensive experience and dedication to our school, Jennifer is our Production Manager and Assistant Director. Working closely with the artistic team, she offers guidance and support to students and crew members, contributing to the success of our performances. Her creative insight and organizational skills make her a precious asset to our artistic community.

Eliza

Eliza, a vocal coach from Brazil, brings over a decade of singing experience to our musical theatre school. With a focus on foundational vocal techniques and individual creativity, Eliza empowers students to embrace their unique voices and storytelling abilities. She fosters a supportive learning environment where students grow not only as performers but also as confident individuals. Passionate about inspiring the next generation of artists, Eliza's dedication and expertise make her an invaluable mentor at our school.

Mark

Mark is an experienced dancer and dedicated instructor from Madrid, Spain at our musical theatre school. With over a decade of experience and training in various dance styles, including classical ballet and contemporary jazz, Mark brings a dynamic teaching approach to his role. He mentors students with enthusiasm, emphasizing discipline and dedication while fostering a supportive environment for artistic growth. Mark's passion for dance and commitment to his students make him a beloved figure in the school community.

Sade

Hailing from the vibrant theatre scene of Lagos, Nigeria, Sade brings a dynamic flair to our school as an esteemed acting teacher. With a career spanning both stage and screen, Sade has honed her craft in traditional Nigerian theatre as well as modern acting techniques. Her passion for storytelling and cultural authenticity infuses her teaching style, inspiring students to explore diverse narratives and embrace their heritage on stage. Sade's commitment to fostering creativity and self-expression in her students reflects her belief in the transformative power of theatre.

Matteo

Originating from the captivating landscapes of Italy, Matteo graces our musical theater institution as the distinguished pianist. With dexterous fingers gracefully gliding across the ivory keys, Matteo breathes vitality into the timeless melodies of Italian classical compositions within our rehearsal spaces. Cultivated in the esteemed conservatories nestled in Milan, Matteo's ardor for the piano resonates as warmly as the Italian sun. His musical voyage has traversed from the majestic concert halls of Rome to the cozy stages of Florence, captivating audiences with his virtuosity and ardor for artistic expression. Embark with Matteo on a musical expedition through Italy's enchanting harmonies and uncover the profound impact of music within the realm of theater.
